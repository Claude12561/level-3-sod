About CPEC Saint Babeth

Founded in 2015 in Gicumbi District, CPEC Saint Babeth is a private educational institution committed to bridging the gap between theoretical knowledge and industry demands. We offer Ordinary Level general education and advanced technical diplomas in Software Development and Multimedia.

Our mission: empower students with critical thinking, digital creativity, and technical proficiency. With small class sizes, experienced instructors, and modern infrastructure in Gicumbi District, we ensure personalized attention.
